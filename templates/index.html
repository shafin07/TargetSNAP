What it does

TargetSNAP analyses the genomic context around your SNP for gain or loss of conserved miRNA seed-match sites (8mer, 7mer-m8, 7mer-A1, 6mer).

🔴
Loss of Function (LOF)

The alternate allele destroys a seed-match site present in the reference sequence — the gene escapes miRNA repression at that site.

🟢
Gain of Function (GOF)

The alternate allele creates a new seed-match site absent in the reference sequence — the gene gains a new miRNA regulatory interaction.
